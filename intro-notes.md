# Introduction to Cybersecurity

## What is Cybersecurity
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from attacks or unauthorized access.

## Key Concepts

### CIA Triad
- Confidentiality → Data is only accessible to authorized users
- Integrity → Data cannot be altered without permission
- Availability → Systems and data must be accessible when needed

## Types of Attacks
- Phishing
- Brute force attacks
- Malware
